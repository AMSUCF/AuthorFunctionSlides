Maniac Mansion Room Concepts

Each new content block gets a distinct room inspired by Maniac Mansion's iconic spaces. Click the ones that feel right for their content.

The Library

For: Foucault & Ted Nelson quotes

Deep purple-brown palette. Towering bookshelves, candelabra, checkered floor. The mansion's seat of knowledge — perfect for foundational theory.

Palette: #1a0a2e, #4a3366, #5c3a1e, #ffcc44

The Kitchen

Warm olive-green and wood tones. Workbench feel — the place where things get made by hand. "Artisanal" craft theme maps to the kitchen's domestic labor.

Dr. Fred's Laboratory

For: Claude as author (claude_author, claude_process, p5.js animation)

The Basement / Dungeon

For: Agents authoring together (OpenClaw, Moltbook, Claw Republic)

Deep crimson-black with purple undertones. Stone walls, chains, glowing eyes in the dark. Where the tentacle lives — where autonomous agents do their unsupervised work.

Palette: #1a0a0a, #3a1a1a, #ff4444, #6a2a6a

The Observatory

For: New ending / looking forward

Deep midnight blue with starfield. Telescope, dome opening to the sky. Looking outward and upward — a reflective, hopeful counterpoint to the ruins room. Where you end up after surviving the mansion.

Palette: #000814, #001d3d, #003566, #ffd60a

How these differ from existing rooms

The current deck uses 14 room templates with palettes centered on warm earth tones (lecture hall, study), cold blues (data center), and muted greens (archive). These Maniac Mansion rooms push into territory the deck hasn't touched:

  • Library — deep purple, distinct from the warm brown study and the blue-toned archive
  • Kitchen — olive-green with wood, unlike any current room
  • Laboratory — cyan/teal glow on navy, cooler and more alien than the data center
  • Basement — crimson-black, the darkest and most foreboding palette in the deck
  • Observatory — midnight blue with gold accents, a brighter, more open feel than any current room
