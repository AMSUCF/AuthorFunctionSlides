Room Layouts with Object Placement

Each room shown at 16:9 ratio with object zones marked. Rooms need clear wall space (upper half) for monitors/posters and surface space for books/objects.

Current pattern (for reference)

Existing rooms follow a consistent layout: floor in bottom ~30%, wall detail in middle band, upper wall/ceiling is open space for hanging objects. The sprite walks along the floor line. Objects sit in 3 zones:

  • Wall zone (top 10-55%): monitor-screen, wall-poster, wall-text — hang on the wall
  • Surface zone (40-70%): book-pedestal, ipad-screen — sit on furniture
  • Floor zone (bottom 30%): sprite walks here, rarely has objects
