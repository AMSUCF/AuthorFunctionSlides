The Typing Office

For: Track Changes / "every tool changes our writing"

Warm brown wood paneling, green banker's lamp casting a pool of light on an old typewriter. Framed diplomas, a worn rug. The room where writing was once a physical, mechanical act — before it became digital.

Palette: #2e2820, #4a4030, #3a8a4a (lamp), #f0e8d8 (paper)

The Observatory

For: New ending / looking forward

Midnight blue with starfield and telescope. Looking outward — a reflective, hopeful counterpoint to the ruins. Where you end up after surviving the mansion.
